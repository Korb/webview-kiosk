## Changes

- feat: device owner capability (enable lock task mode instead of screen pinning)
- feat: introduce kiosk control panel to allow custom unlock
  - Allow tapping screen 10-times at the top-left corner (default) region
    of the screen to open the kiosk control panel
  - Region is configurable - top-left, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right,
    top, bottom, full, and disabled
  - In the panel, users can select "Forward", "Backward", "Home", "Refresh" or
    "Unlock" (if in locked mode - biometrics or password will be required).
